I've removed broken mods and placed ones that aren't used normally inside special groups away from the main mods. This is not my cheat table and I can't credit the author beyond the name "Nathan" in the scripts' headers because I'm not even sure where I got this table from originally.

## Known quirks

### Halo CE:
Increase jump height is finnicky and can make your jump so high that you launch "into space" lol, be careful with it

### Halo 2:
"Infinite shield" carries over to unmodded playthroughs after save is made. For example, if you play with the mods and turn infinite shield on, when you save and quit and reload the game, you will still have MANY layers of overshield. It's no longer infinite, but you can get shot in LASO for around 60 seconds before you actually die. This is very valuable in Cairo Station at the end.

### Halo 3:
Not much to say about this one except the aimbot is cracked, the best of the series here.
